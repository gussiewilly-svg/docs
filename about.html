Who We Are

Built by Vermonters,
for Vermonters.

Champlain Junk Removal was founded in 2026 with one simple idea: junk removal should be honest, straightforward, and fair. No bait-and-switch pricing. No crews who don't show up. Just reliable people with a truck and a commitment to doing right by their neighbors.

We're based right here in Vergennes, in the heart of Addison County. We serve the communities we know best — Middlebury, Burlington, Shelburne, Ferrisburgh, and the towns in between. Close to home means faster response, better pricing, and neighbors helping neighbors.
